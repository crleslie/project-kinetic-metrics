Project Kinetic Metrics

An open-source data initiative tracking ammunition pricing, ballistics, and market availability to build better tools for hunters and researchers.

Research Goals

  • 1
    Ballistic Tradeoffs

    Mapping the mathematical relationships between power factor, downrange energy, trajectory, and cost per round.

  • 2
    Economic Efficiency

    Identifying the most economical calibers suitable for specific big game species without sacrificing ethical performance.

  • 3
    The "Green Tax"

    Quantifying the price discrepancy between traditional lead-core rounds and similarly performing lead-free (monolithic) alternatives.

  • 4
    Market Availability

    Tracking which big game calibers maintain the most consistent stock across major US retailers.
