Project Kinetic Metrics

An open-source data initiative tracking ammunition pricing, ballistics, and market availability to build better tools for hunters and researchers.

How to Use the Bookmarklet

Initial Setup

  1. Make your Bookmark Bar visible (Ctrl+Shift+B or Cmd+Shift+B).
  2. Drag the button above directly into your bookmarks bar.

Data Collection Workflow

Step 1: Gather on One Retailer

Navigate through different ammunition boxes on a single retailer's site (e.g., MidwayUSA). On each page, click the bookmarklet and hit Save Entry to log the data.

Step 2: Export Before You Leave

Once you are finished with that retailer, click CSV in the panel to download your dataset. Data cannot transfer across different websites; saving data from more than one site will cause issues.

Step 3: Reset for the Next Site

Before navigating to a completely different retailer's website, click the red Clear button twice to reset the counter to zero. You are now ready to repeat Step 1 on the next site.

Research Goals

  • 1
    Ballistic Essentials

    Comparing ballistic performance such as muzzle velocity, recoil, and downrange ballistics to find the best performance options for your rifle setup.

  • 2
    Field Application

    Helping consumers balance price with practical field performance - optimizing both terminal harvest capability and shootability for specific game and hunting styles.

  • 3
    The Lead-Free Premium

    Measuring the actual retail price difference between traditional lead-core bullets and similarly performing monolithic copper alternatives.

  • 4
    Retail Availability & Price

    Tracking which calibers offer more load, manufacturer, stock availability, and pricing options across major US retailers.
