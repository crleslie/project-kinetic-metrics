Ammo Price Scraper Bookmarklet

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar to install the scraping tool. Includes advanced specs (UPC, Ballistics) and Shadow DOM protection.

AmmoScraper v2.1.3

Instructions:

  1. Make your Bookmark Bar visible (Ctrl+Shift+B or Cmd+Shift+B).
  2. Drag the button above into the bar.
  3. Navigate to a specific box of ammo on a supported retailer's site.
  4. Click the bookmarklet to open the extractor panel. Review, edit if needed, and hit Save Entry.
  5. Important: Because browsers isolate memory per website, you must click CSV to download your data before moving to a different retailer's website!
  6. Clear Data: When switching to a new retailer's website, clear your previously saved data by clicking the red Clear button in the extractor panel twice.
